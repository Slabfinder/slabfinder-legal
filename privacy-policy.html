Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Welcome to SlabFinder, owned and operated by Poseidon Industries Inc. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using SlabFinder, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register for and use SlabFinder, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile Information: Profile photo uploaded through our service
  • Business Information: Company name, business logo, business address, business description, and operating hours
  • Listing Information: Photos of stone slabs/blocks, material descriptions, dimensions, prices, and location information
  • Payment Information: Subscription plan selected (processed through Apple/Google payment systems - we do not store credit card information)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including listing views, search queries, and interactions with other users' profiles
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Analytics Data: App performance data, crash reports, and usage statistics collected through Firebase Analytics

1.3 Location Information

We collect location information only when you manually enter it for your business or listings. We do not track your GPS location or collect automatic geolocation data.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process your subscription and manage billing
  • Enable you to create, publish, and manage listings
  • Connect buyers and sellers through our marketplace
  • Display your public business profile to other users

2.2 Communication

  • Send transactional emails (account verification, subscription confirmations, listing status updates)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send important service announcements and updates

2.3 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality and user experience
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Public Information

The following information is publicly visible to all app users:

  • Your business name and description
  • Business logo and profile photo
  • Listing photos and details (materials, prices, dimensions)
  • Business location (if provided)
  • Subscription plan badge (Buyer, Seller Basic, or Seller Pro)

3.2 Service Providers

We share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Cloud database, authentication, and analytics
  • Cloudinary: Image hosting and optimization for photos
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: Subscription payment processing
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and analytics

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Location

Your data is primarily stored in the United States (us-central1 region) through Google's Firebase infrastructure.

4.2 Security Measures

  • Access controls and monitoring
